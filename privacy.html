Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Files you upload for processing (temporarily, deleted immediately after processing)
  • Usage statistics and analytics (anonymized)
  • Credit usage information (stored locally in your browser)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process your files and provide our PDF tools
  • Track your credit usage and daily limits
  • Process payments for additional credits
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All file uploads are processed securely and deleted immediately after processing
  • No files are stored permanently on our servers
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Payment information is processed securely through Stripe

4. Data Retention

We do not retain your files or personal data beyond what is necessary:

  • Uploaded files are deleted immediately after processing
  • Credit usage data is stored locally in your browser
  • Payment records are retained by Stripe according to their policies
  • Analytics data is anonymized and retained for service improvement

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (anonymized)
  • CDN Services: For fast content delivery

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (minimal data is collected)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@webspind.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
