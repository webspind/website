Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an "action"?

An action is any completed operation: merging PDFs, converting images, generating QR codes, or analyzing text. OCR and AI detection count as 2 actions due to their computational intensity.

When do my actions reset?

Actions reset daily at UTC midnight. You'll see a countdown timer showing when your next reset occurs.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Pro Pass subscriptions can be cancelled anytime. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

What's included in Lifetime?

Lifetime includes all Pro features forever with a one-time payment. It's limited to web-only access and has limited availability.

Is my data still private?

Absolutely! All processing still happens locally in your browser. We never see or store your files, regardless of your plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe. Your payment information is secure and never stored on our servers.

We're here to help! Contact us if you need assistance with your plan or have any questions.
