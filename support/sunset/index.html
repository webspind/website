Frequently Asked Questions

What is "Did I Earn The Sunset"? +

"Did I Earn The Sunset" is a mindful journaling app that helps you reflect on your day by asking whether you've earned the beautiful sunset you're witnessing. It's designed to promote gratitude, self-reflection, and appreciation for life's simple pleasures.

How do I start journaling? +

Simply open the app and begin writing about your day. The app will guide you through prompts to help you reflect on what you've accomplished, what you're grateful for, and whether you feel you've earned the sunset. There's no right or wrong way to journal - just be honest with yourself.

Can I access my journal entries offline? +

Yes! All your journal entries are stored locally on your device, so you can write and read your reflections even without an internet connection. Your entries will sync when you're back online.

Is my journal data private and secure? +

Absolutely. Your journal entries are private and stored securely on your device. We don't have access to your personal reflections, and your data is encrypted to ensure maximum privacy and security.

What if I miss a day of journaling? +

That's perfectly okay! Journaling is about self-care, not perfection. You can always go back and write about a previous day, or simply start fresh the next day. The app is designed to be flexible and supportive of your journey.

Can I export my journal entries? +

Yes, you can export your journal entries in various formats (PDF, text, or JSON) to keep a backup or share your reflections. This feature helps you maintain ownership of your personal growth journey.
