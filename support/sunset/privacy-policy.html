Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the security of your personal journal entries are our top priority.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

This privacy policy explains how "Did I Earn The Sunset" collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information from you. The app operates entirely locally on your device.

Journal Entries

Your journal entries are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. This includes:

  • Text content of your journal entries
  • Timestamps of when entries were created
  • Any personal reflections or thoughts you write
  • App settings and preferences

Device Information

We do not collect device information, location data, or any other identifying information about your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information or journal entries, we do not use your data in any way. All processing happens locally on your device.

Local Processing Only

  • All journal entries are processed and stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking is performed
  • No third-party services have access to your data

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your journal entries are stored securely on your device using your device's built-in storage mechanisms. This ensures:

  • Complete privacy and control over your data
  • No risk of data breaches from external servers
  • Access to your entries even when offline
  • Data remains on your device unless you choose to export it

Data Encryption

While your data is stored locally, we recommend using your device's built-in security features (such as device passcodes, biometric authentication, or encryption) to protect your journal entries.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your journal entries remain completely private and are never:

  • Sent to our servers
  • Shared with advertisers
  • Transmitted to analytics services
  • Used for marketing purposes
  • Accessed by third-party applications

5. Your Rights and Control

Complete Control

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device. You can:

  • View, edit, or delete any journal entry at any time
  • Export your entries in various formats (PDF, text, JSON)
  • Delete the app and all associated data
  • Backup your data using your device's backup features

Data Export

The app provides built-in export functionality that allows you to create backups of your journal entries in multiple formats, ensuring you maintain full ownership of your personal reflections.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with all applicable children's privacy laws, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Parents and guardians can be confident that their children's journal entries remain completely private and are never transmitted or shared.

7. International Users

This privacy policy applies to all users regardless of location. Since no data is transmitted to external servers, your privacy is protected regardless of your country of residence.

We comply with international privacy regulations including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and other applicable privacy laws.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the app's privacy practices, please contact us:

Webspind Support

  • Email: support@webspind.com
  • Website: https://webspind.com/support/sunset
  • Response Time: Usually within 24 hours

10. App Store Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to meet the requirements of major app stores including:

  • Apple App Store: Complies with Apple's privacy guidelines and requirements
  • Google Play Store: Meets Google's data safety requirements
  • Other Platforms: Designed to meet general privacy policy standards
